Jurassic Escapade

Bio-Engineering Club

In Jurassic Escapade the room will be divided into 4 zones. The team is in a safe shed which lies in the middle of all these zones. Their goal is to solve various puzzles in each of the zones which completes 4 different elements of escaping. While the team explores through different zones, one or more zones will randomly keep turning red which indicates that dinosaurs are in the area. While a zone is red, the team will not be able to work in that particular region for a certain region. A journal will be provided with clues. Use of electronic equipment is not allowed. If a member stays in the red zone for more than 5 seconds, they will be considered dead and will be disqualified.
